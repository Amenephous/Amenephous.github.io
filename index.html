About Me

A prospective Mechatronics and Robotics Master's graduate from NYU, expecting to complete my degree in May 2024. My passion lies in the realm of robotics, where I aim to leverage my skills in C++, Python, and Matlab programming alongside expertise in machine vision to contribute to the evolving landscape of robotics technology. Throughout my academic journey, I've immersed myself in diverse projects aimed at honing my skills and understanding of robotics.

From tinkering with the Unitree Go1-Pro robotdog and exploring Visual SLAM techniques as a research assistant to annotating and labelling data for deep learning models at Building Diagnostic Robotics, Inc., I've continued to advance my skills. At Photoshooto, I led teams and managed time effectively, while my internship at Reliance Corporate Park provided hands-on experience in industrial automation. Each role has shaped my commitment to pushing the boundaries of robotics and technology.

Raised in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, I appreciate diverse cultures and aim to make a positive impact. my goal is to play a pivotal role in advancing the capabilities of robotics technology, ultimately enhancing efficiency and productivity across industries.
